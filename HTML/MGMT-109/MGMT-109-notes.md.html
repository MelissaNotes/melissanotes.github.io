A. Exporting – shipment of goods to foreign buyer in their country

  1. TYPES: Direct & Indirect
    • Direct: Sell directly to foreign buyer or agent.
      Eg. Apple sells Iphone to people in Beijing
    • Indirect: Sell to 3rd party broker, who in turn sells to foreign buyer.
      Eg. Apple sells Iphone to distributors in Beijing who will sell phones to people later
